A user signs up and picks their primary team (most will pick Product). To access Finance agents, they add the Finance team for $5/mo more.

Strategic Implications

Pros:

  • Natural upsell path (land with Product, expand to Finance, Legal, etc.)
  • Clear value per team â€” users pay for what they use

Cons:
  • Complexity in billing (team-level granularity)

    The CEO Question

    Should we have a CEO agent?

    A CEO agent would handle:

    • Company-wide strategic planning
    • Cross-functional prioritization (when Product, Finance, and Legal disagree)
    • Board meeting preparation and narratives
    • Culture and values articulation
    • Investor/stakeholder communication strategy
    • Company-level OKR/goal setting
    • Risk assessment across all functions
    Argument FOR: CEO is the natural apex. In a "Full Organization" deployment, someone needs to be the ultimate synthesizer. The PLT gateway currently serves this purpose but doesn't have a single accountable perspective â€” it's a committee. A CEO agent provides that single-point strategic view.

    Argument AGAINST: Most Legionis users won't be actual CEOs. A CPO or VP Product is a more natural primary user. CEO might feel presumptuous.

    My recommendation: Yes, include CEO â€” but position as the "Strategic Advisor" that synthesizes across all functions. Available in "Full Organization" tier only.
