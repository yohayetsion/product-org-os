# Product Organization Asset Blueprint

Product organizations produce and maintain these core assets:

| # | Asset | Owner | Purpose | V2V Phase | Related Skills |

| 1 | Product Vision & Roadmap | VP Product / Dir PM | Strategic direction | 1, 3 | `/vision-statement`, `/product-roadmap`, `/roadmap-theme` |
| 2 | Product Delivery Planning | Dir PM / PM | Execution coordination | 4 | `/launch-readiness`, `/launch-plan` |
| 3 | Product Requirements | Dir PM / PM | Feature definition | 3, 4 | `/prd`, `/feature-spec`, `/user-story` |
| 4 | Pricing Strategy | VP Product / BizOps | Revenue optimization | 2 | `/pricing-strategy`, `/pricing-model` |
| 5 | Business Plan | BizOps | Business case | 2 | `/business-case`, `/business-plan` |
| 6 | Go to Market | Dir PMM | Market entry | 3, 4 | `/gtm-strategy`, `/gtm-brief`, `/sales-enablement` |
| 7 | Market & Customer Intimacy | CI / PMM | Customer insights | 1, 5 | `/market-analysis`, `/competitive-landscape`, `/feedback-capture` |
| 8 | Organizational Processes | Product Ops | Ways of working | Cross | `/decision-charter`, `/escalation-rule` |
| 9 | Stakeholder Intimacy | Dir PM / Dir PMM | Relationship management | Cross | `/stakeholder-brief`, `/strategy-communication` |
| 10 | Value Realization | Value Realization | Outcome tracking | 5, 6 | `/value-realization-report`, `/customer-health-scorecard`, `/outcome-review` |

## Asset Details

### 1. Product Vision & Roadmap

**Deliverables**:
- Product vision document
- Multi-horizon roadmap
- Theme definitions
- Initiative plans

**Quality Criteria**:
- Aligned to company strategy
- Customer-value focused
- Realistic and achievable
- Clearly communicated

### 2. Product Delivery Planning

**Deliverables**:
- Release plans
- Sprint/iteration plans
- Capacity planning
- Dependency maps

**Quality Criteria**:
- Predictable delivery
- Dependencies managed
- Risks identified
- Stakeholders aligned

### 3. Product Requirements

**Deliverables**:
- PRDs
- Feature specs
- User stories
- Acceptance criteria

**Quality Criteria**:
- Customer problem focused
- Testable criteria
- Clear scope
- Prioritized

### 4. Pricing Strategy

**Deliverables**:
- Pricing model
- Price points
- Packaging strategy
- Discount policy

**Quality Criteria**:
- Value-aligned
- Competitive
- Profitable
- Scalable

### 5. Business Plan

**Deliverables**:
- Business cases
- Financial projections
- Investment requests
- QBR materials

**Quality Criteria**:
- Evidence-based
- Assumptions explicit
- ROI clear
- Risks addressed

### 6. Go to Market

**Deliverables**:
- GTM strategy
- Launch plans
- Sales enablement
- Marketing campaigns

**Quality Criteria**:
- Positioning clear
- Channels identified
- Metrics defined
- Cross-functional aligned

### 7. Market & Customer Intimacy

**Deliverables**:
- Market analysis
- Competitive intelligence
- Customer research
- Win/loss analysis

**Quality Criteria**:
- Current and accurate
- Actionable insights
- Accessible to team
- Regularly updated

### 8. Organizational Processes

**Deliverables**:
- Process documentation
- Templates
- Tools and systems
- Training materials

**Quality Criteria**:
- Enables not constrains
- Appropriate to maturity
- Adopted by team
- Continuously improved

### 9. Stakeholder Intimacy

**Deliverables**:
- Stakeholder maps
- Communication plans
- Meeting cadences
- Relationship tracking

**Quality Criteria**:
- Key stakeholders identified
- Regular engagement
- Feedback incorporated
- Trust maintained

### 10. Value Realization

**Deliverables**:
- Success metrics
- Adoption tracking
- ROI analysis
- Outcome reviews

**Quality Criteria**:
- Metrics defined pre-launch
- Tracking automated
- Reviews conducted
- Learnings applied
